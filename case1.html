Uncertain Time

The future is a time of great technological advances, and social robots are a big part of that. This piece is called the "Uncertain Time" the first of a four piece collection. Here the artist tries to depict an interim stage where some people are using the robots to fly, whereas some people are still choosing to live with the older style of living.

As Zdzisław Beksiński, a Polish painter, photographer, and sculptor, specializing in the field of dystopian surrealism, once mentioned in one of his interviews that he hates when people try to symbolically interpret the paintings, rather he would appreciate more when viewers would try to comprehend the "mood and atmosphere" of the art to describe the issues and situation.

Similar to that, this artwork also follows the dark atmosphere which is relevant as the growth of technology and exponential automation might lead to loss of vegetation. Therefore this artwork focuses on both sides of a future with increased dependency on autonomous social robots. This is a more realistic view of the future, where technology has made life more easier yet so polluted.
