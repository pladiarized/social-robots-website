Rest for the Wicked

The last piece of the collection is titled "Rest for the Wicked" which explores multi faceted nature of the future and interdependence on social. In the painting the artist, paints a robot resting in the middle of a marketplace. This is a new concept which is also unfamiliar to many as most of us in this contemporary world believes robots can work 24/7 without resting. This artwork proposes a new perspective.

In this painting, the robot is resting and observing the surroundings. The artist also paints multiple human figure in the background, which seems to be minding their business in the marketplace. In this marketplace, it's evident that people have been accustomed to giant robots there. Moreover, again this artwork presents a similar future inspired by technologies yet the atmosphere is relatively like medieval era.

This painting makes the viewer think about the various ways in which technology can shape the future, and opens up new possibilities for how we might interact with robots. It also makes us think about the role of robots in society, and how they might one day be integrated into our everyday lives.
